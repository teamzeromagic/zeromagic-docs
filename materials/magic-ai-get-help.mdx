The Magic AI - Get Help provides helpful assistance based on user prompts. This feature enables developers to seek guidance on various topics, from API development to GraphQL and RESTful APIs.

## How to use?

- Click on `Get Help` located in the top right corner of the page. This will open field to input your prompt. 

- Simply enter your query, and our AI will provide you with the information you need.
